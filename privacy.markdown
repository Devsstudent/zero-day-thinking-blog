# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Orson's Blog. This privacy policy explains how we collect and use your data when you visit this website.

## Information We Collect
Since this is a static blog hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not directly collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers unless you explicitly provide them through a contact form or email.

## Log Data and Analytics
We may use standard analytics tools (like Google Analytics or GitHub Pages internal metrics) that collect anonymous, aggregate data. This log data may include:
- Your IP address
- Browser type and version
- The pages you visit
- The time and date of your visit

## External Links
Our blog contains links to external sites (like GitHub, LinkedIn, and X). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit, as we have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites or services.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at exactly the email provided in the footer.
